Video Preparation Overview

This chapter provides an an overview of video preparation for a 3DO title. Keep in mind that the source data in video processing is raw, uncompressed video, film, or film converted to video, probably at 640 x 480 pixel resolution. The goal of video processing is to produce a cropped, resized, compressed movie that is ready for data streaming.

Below is a list of the major steps in the process, and the corresponding chapter in this document. Figure 1 shows a flow chart of the process.

  • Selecting hardware
  • Preparing source material
  • Capturing from video
  • Preprocessing digitized materials (deinterlacing and/or 3-2 Pulldown)
  • Processing materials (cropping, resizing, and processing audio)
  • Preparing for Cinepak (or other) compression
